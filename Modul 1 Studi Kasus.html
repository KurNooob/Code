Alhaitham is one of the newly added characters in the 3.4 Genshin Impact update, wielding a sword along with the Dendro Vision. Alhaitham is a member of the Haravatat of the Sumeru Akademiya and the Akademiya's Scribe, responsible for documenting their findings and drafting ordinances. The current scribe of the Sumeru Akademiya. This title may sound very impressive, but it's really because the Akademiya has a culture of using official titles to inflate one's ego. He is a capable person who keeps a low profile for too long is often perceived as someone with a mysterious identity and ulterior purposes.
